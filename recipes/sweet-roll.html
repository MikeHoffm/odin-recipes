Dragonborns Sweet Roll

Description

What's wrong, did someone steal your sweet roll? Well if that's the case then you're in luck. This delicious pastry will have all of your friends shouting as if they'd been trained by the greybeards themselves!

Ingridients

Sweet Rolls

  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter (room temp)
  • 1 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 2/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs

Icing/Glaze

  • 1 teaspon of vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 2 cups of confectioner's sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F. WIth non-stick spray, grease a mini bundt cake pan and set aside.
  2. In a stand-mixer with the paddle attachment, cream the sugar and butter for 5 minutes until light and fluffy
  3. Add in one egg at a time, beating after each addition. Mix in your heavy cream until blended, and scrape the sides of the bowl as needed.
  4. Add in baking powder, salt, vanilla, and flour and mix on low until combined.
  5. Pour your batter into the bundt pan, and bake for 20 mins or until golden around the bottom edges. Allow the cake to cool for 5 minutes before setting on a cooking rack
  6. For the icing, melt your butter in a microwavable bowl before adding in the milk. Mix in the confectioner's sugar and vinalla slowly. Drizzle over the cooled sweet rolls and enjoy!
