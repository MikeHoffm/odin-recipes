Grandmas Cookies

Description

A soft and warm cookie can bring a smile to anyones face. The recipe for these delicious chocolate chip cookies won't be hard to do. Actually, may already have most of the ingridients on hand in your kitchen already. Once all of the ingridients have been mixed together properly, it will be a short wait before you have yourself the perfect batch.

Ingridients

  • 3/4 cups of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspon of salt
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 (3 ounce) pack of instant white chocolate pudding mix
  • 1 cup of botter, softened
  • 2 teaspons of vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degreef F (190 degrees C). Mix the baking soda, salt, instant pudding powder, and flower together. Set this mixture to the side for now
  2. In a large to medium bowl, cream the brown sugar, white sugar, and butter together until it is smooth. Blend in your vanila and eggs. Mix in the dry ingridients gradually until blended. Stir the chocolate chips into the mix using a wooden spoon. Scoop tablespoon sizes of the cookie mixture onto an ungreased baking sheet. Make sure that your cookies are at least 2 inches apart from one another.
  3. Place the baking sheet inside the oven, and allow it to bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden. Let them cool for a few minutes on the baking sheet before serving.
