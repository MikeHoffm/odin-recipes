Skywalker Farms Blue Milk

Description

Whether you're a optomistic blonde teen with daddy issues, or just over paying a certain Mouse to indulge in your childhood fantasies... You'll love this refreshing drink froma galaxy far far away! Traditionally blue milk (Bantha Milk) is obtained through the glands of a female Bantha. Unfortunately Banthas may be a little hard to come by in our sector, so here's a fun alternative!

Ingridients

  • 1/4 teaspon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of blue fruit punch powder
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • blue food dye (as needed)

Steps

  1. In a blender combine ice, vanilla, fruit punch powder, and milk. Cover and blend until smooth.
  2. Mix in a couple of drops of the blue food dye until you achieve your desired color.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
